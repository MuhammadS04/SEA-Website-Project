My Journey

My passion for the gym started around age 15 when I would see the change it brings to peoples' lives. I started by doing pushups in my room until I got really good at them. Shortly after, I started to the gym with my uncle which introduced me to real workout equipment. From there, my love for the gym skyrocketed and I found myself joining my highschool wrestling team and weight room. Overall I have been familiar with the gym for about 4 years, and consistently working out for about 2. This website shares some of my current favorite workouts.
